Overview

Purpose

This is a website that provides recreational activities on free flowing rivers. We provide all the safety requirement for our recreationalist, and ensures they have a remarkable, memorable time with us. We use an inflatable raft to navigate rivers or other body of waters; especially whitewaters. Some are rough, but the risk is part of the experience. Enjoy

Audience

"Our water rafting website targets adventure enthusiasts, thrill-seekers, and nature lovers who are passionate about exploring rivers, experiencing adrenaline-pumping activities, and immersing themselves in the great outdoors."

Question 1: Who are the target customers? Families with young children are seeking a fun and safe activity for their vacation.
Our website showcases family-friendly rafting routes suitable for all ages, accompanied by expert guides trained in ensuring a safe and enjoyable experience. We provide FAQs addressing common concerns parents may have, such as safety protocols, equipment sizing for kids, and nearby amenities like restrooms and picnic areas.

Question 2:How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)? Experienced adventure seekers are searching for challenging rapids and off-the-beaten-path destinations.
Our website is available on all devices both laptop, desktop, and mobile device. Our developer team has ensured that our website is accessible in all browsers with high speed loading time.

Question 3:What do they want? A group of college students is planning a summer adventure trip.
Our website offers customizable rafting packages suitable for groups, along with detailed information about safety measures, gear rentals, and nearby accommodation options. Additionally, we feature testimonials from previous student groups highlighting their thrilling experiences.
